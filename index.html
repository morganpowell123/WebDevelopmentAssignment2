About

The Gloucestershire Academy is one of seven bodies of teachers that delivers the 3rd, 4th and 5th year of the University of Bristol Undergraduate Medical Student Curriculum. It is the largest provider outside Bristol itself.

All eleven units of the curriculum are delivered in part or in total and all parts of the Primary Care Service, the Gloucestershire NHS Foundation Hospitals Trust and the Gloucestershire 2Gether Trust is used in doing so. Every unit of the curriculum is led locally by a unit co-ordinator and the unit is delivered through unit tutors and a body of teachers, both doctors and other professionals, relevant to that unit. The unit co-ordinators link with the unit leads at the University and the unit co-ordinators from the other six academies. Hence the provision of education for student doctors by the University of Bristol in Gloucestershire is designed to be consistent with what is envisaged centrally and what is delivered within the other 6 academies.

Cloisters at Gloucester Cathedral The University of Bristol Medical Dean in Gloucestershire is Dr Phil Davies. Our Undergraduate Medical Education Manager is Angie Coulson with administrative support in the university offices provided by a team of 2 Administrators and 3 Administration Assistants. The University of Bristol Offices are located in Redwood Education Centre on the Gloucestershire Royal Hospital campus but the University Administrator can also be found on Tuesdays at the Sandford Education Centre in Cheltenham. Further Academy contacts can be found here: Academy Contacts

As well as delivering the curriculum, the clinicians in Gloucestershire also participate in the Objective Structured Clinical Examination (OSCE) finals examination at Bristol and the Yr4 OSCE’s held at Sandford Education Centre, Cheltenham in June each year. They also join with colleagues from the other academies to examine the final Objective Structured Long case Examination Record (OSLER) examinations in Gloucester.

Facilities

Accomodation in Gloucestershire

Accommodation for students in Gloucester is provided by Markey in conjunction with the Hospital Trusts accommodation provider, Spectrum Housing Group. All of our accommodation is no more than a 10-15 minute walk from Gloucestershire Royal Hospital.

Formal Place

A 23 bedroom property situated on Southgate Street, Gloucester accommodating our 5th year students. The property is split into a mix of 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property although there is an NCP car park at the rear of the accommodation.

> <

Henley Court

A 16 bedroom property situated on Montpellier Place, Gloucester accommodating 4th year students. The property is split into 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property.

> <

Whitfield House

A 61 bed property situated on Whitfield Street, Gloucester accommodating a mix of 3rd and 4th year students. The property is split into a mix of 1, 2, 3, 4 & 6 bed flats all with shared shower rooms, kitchen/diner and lounge area.

> <

All of the above properties in Gloucester are newly furnished with Bed, Wardrobe, Drawer Unit, Desk & Chair and with Fridge & Freezer, Microwave, Toaster, Kettle, Ironing Boards (but NOT irons), vacuum cleaners, dining tables, chairs and soft seating. You will need to bring basic cooking utensils, crockery, cutlery, pots & pans (although a limited supply may be available), duvet, pillows, bed linen and towels as they are not provided.

We encourage you to use the vacuum cleaners regularly during your stays. Please ensure that when you use the vacuums that you empty the dirt containers regularly and report any faults to the Undergraduate Office immediately, so that we can investigate/solve the problem promptly for you.

Accommodation in Cheltenham

Accomodation for students in Cheltenham is provided by the Hospital Trusts accomodation provider, Spectrum Housing Grou. All of our accommodation is no more than a 10-15 minute walk from Cheltenham General Hospital.

Formal Place

A 23 bedroom property situated on Southgate Street, Gloucester accommodating our 5th year students. The property is split into a mix of 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property although there is an NCP car park at the rear of the accommodation.

> <

Henley Court

A 16 bedroom property situated on Montpellier Place, Gloucester accommodating 4th year students. The property is split into 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property.

> <

5th years studying in Cheltenham will be accommodated in Caduceus House, Christowe Lane (a 16 bed property split into four 4 bedroom flats) and 4th years studying in Cheltenham will be accommodated in two 6 bedroom houses in Sandford Mill Road. These properties are managed by the Trust accommodation provider, Spectrum Housing Group and come fully furnished. These properties also come with a housekeeping service once a week for communal areas and bathrooms and laundry service (for bed linen that has been provided only) on a weekly basis. Each house in Sandford Mill Road has two off road parking spaces only.

All of our accommodation in Cheltenham is fully furnished with Bed, Wardrobe, Chest of Drawers, Desk, Chair, Bedside Table and Lamp and with Fridge/Freezer, Microwave, Kettle, Ironing Boards and irons, basic cooking utensils, pans and crockery. PLEASE NOTE : You will be provided with a set of bed linen and a towel but you may wish to bring extra bed linen and towels with you.
